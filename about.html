About

Founded by Rana

Rana

ShadhiFilmer was born from a bold creative choice. In his third year pursuing a Bachelor of Arts, Rana stepped away from the conventional academic path to follow an undeniable calling — photography as an art form, not merely a profession.

What began as a freelance journey soon transformed into a global narrative. With experience across 150+ weddings, including refined destination celebrations in Dubai, Rana developed a signature style shaped by international exposure, cultural diversity, and an uncompromising eye for detail.

Today, ShadhiFilmer stands as a luxury wedding photography studio, specializing in fine-art and editorial storytelling for modern couples who seek elegance, depth, and authenticity.
